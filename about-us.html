Who Is Edelman Insurance Management Group?

Edelman Insurance Management Group was founded in 1996 by Scott Edelman. At Edelman Insurance Management Group, we work hard to bring the knowledge and experience of an insurance professional to you. We provide products and services for insurances of our clients, thier families, retirees, and businesses. We have a strong commitment to giving quality advise and service. We put an emphasis on creating lasting relationships and regard each of our clients as a friend.

Learn More About Scott.

Scott is a qualifying and life member of Million Dollar Round Table (MDRT) - for which membership is recognized internationally as the standard of excellence in the life insurance and financial services industry. He has achieved Top of The Table status at MDRT for the last 8 straight years and Court of The Table for the last 19. He also serves as MDRT's State Chair of Pennsylvania. In addition, he is a member of the National Association of Insurance and Financial Advisors (NAIFA) with 22 straight years of various awards. Scott is a member of the Bucks County Estate Planning Council. He has consistently achieved ACE Platinum Level with Signator Investors, Inc. He has appeared in numerous industry publications including Money Magazine. He was most recently recognized as a Five Start Wealth Manager in Philadelphia Magazine for the seconds year in a row.
